Welcome to Sort My Stash!

Take a good look at this picture of my yarn stash. Does it strike a familiar (and slightly chaotic) chord? Don't worryâ€”you're in the right place!

While Sort My Stash can't declutter the physical piles of yarn in your life (wouldn't that be magical?), it can bring order to the chaos digitally. With just a few clicks, you'll know exactly:

  • What yarn you have.
  • The brand, colour, and fibre content.
  • How much of it is hiding in your stash.
You can even add notes to help locate your treasures in real life, like: "4 balls of Debbie Bliss in the black-lidded plastic box in the loft."

I can't promise Sort My Stash will reduce the size of your yarn stash (let's be honest, that's not really the goal here, is it?), but it will help stop accidental duplicate purchases and ease the guilt of wondering, "What can I do with all this lovely yarn?"

With your stash organized at your fingertips, you can instantly check what you've got and search for the perfect project to bring it to life. Ready to turn yarn chaos into crafting confidence?
